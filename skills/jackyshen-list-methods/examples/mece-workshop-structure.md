# MECE-Applied Workshop Structure Example

## Workshop: Effective Communication Skills

### MECE Module Breakdown

```
Communication Skills Workshop (MECE)
├── Intra-Personal Communication
│   ├── Self-Awareness
│   │   ├── Communication style assessment
│   │   └── Personal strengths/weaknesses
│   └── Self-Management
│       ├── Emotional regulation
│       └── Stress management during communication
│
├── Inter-Personal Communication
│   ├── One-to-One Communication
│   │   ├── Active listening
│   │   ├── Questioning techniques
│   │   └── Feedback delivery
│   └── Group Communication
│       ├── Team presentations
│       ├── Meeting facilitation
│       └── Conflict navigation
│
└── Organizational Communication
    ├── Upward Communication
    │   ├── Reporting to management
    │   └── Proposal presentations
    └── Cross-Functional Communication
        ├── Stakeholder management
        └── Cross-team collaboration
```

### MECE Verification

**Mutually Exclusive Check:**
- [x] No overlap between Intra, Inter, and Organizational
- [x] One-to-One vs Group are distinct
- [x] Upward vs Cross-Functional don't overlap

**Collectively Exhaustive Check:**
- [x] All communication contexts covered
- [x] No major gaps (individual, team, org all included)

### Learning Outcomes by Module (TfBR-aligned)

**Intra-Personal Communication:**
After this session, participants will be able to identify their communication style and regulate emotions during difficult conversations.

**Inter-Personal Communication:**
After this session, participants will be able to demonstrate active listening, deliver constructive feedback, and facilitate group discussions.

**Organizational Communication:**
After this session, participants will be able to present to management effectively and collaborate across functional boundaries.

### Time Allocation (70/30 TfBR Ratio)

```
Total: 6 hours (360 minutes)

Intra-Personal (90 min)
├── Opening & Assessment (20 min) - Activity: Style assessment
├── Content: Self-awareness concepts (10 min)
├── Activity: Emotional triggers practice (30 min)
├── Debrief: ORID (15 min)
└── Action Planning (15 min)

Inter-Personal (150 min)
├── Activity 1: Listening practice (30 min)
├── Content: Active listening model (10 min)
├── Debrief 1 (10 min)
├── Activity 2: Feedback role-plays (40 min)
├── Content: SBI feedback model (10 min)
├── Debrief 2 (15 min)
├── Activity 3: Group facilitation (25 min)
├── Debrief 3 (10 min)

Organizational (120 min)
├── Activity: Present to management (40 min)
├── Content: Executive presentation structure (10 min)
├── Debrief (15 min)
├── Activity: Cross-team collaboration simulation (30 min)
├── Content: Stakeholder mapping (10 min)
├── Debrief (10 min)
└── Closing commitments (5 min)
```

### Content Outline (Minimal)

**Intra-Personal (10 min content):**
1. Communication styles framework (visual model)
2. Emotional trigger patterns

**Inter-Personal (20 min content):**
1. Active listening 3-step model
2. SBI feedback framework
3. Group facilitation principles

**Organizational (20 min content):**
1. Executive presentation structure (Pyramid Principle)
2. Stakeholder mapping tool
